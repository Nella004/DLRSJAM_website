What Is DLRSJAM?

A smarter way to renew your licence — built for Jamaica.

DLRSJAM — the Digital Licence Renewal System Jamaica — is a working prototype developed by five University of the West Indies students as part of the COMP3901 Capstone Project.

The system replaces the traditional, paper-heavy TAJ branch visit with a fully digital process: upload your documents, verify your identity through AI, and receive your digital driver's licence — all from your phone or computer.

The Problem

Why Jamaica needs
a digital solution.

The current TAJ driver's licence renewal process has three major failures that affect thousands of Jamaicans every year.

Problem 01
⏳

Hours Lost in Queues

Every renewal requires a physical visit to a TAJ branch, where long wait times are the norm — wasting time that citizens simply cannot afford to lose.

📍 Affects every single Jamaican driver
Problem 02
🗺️

Rural Inaccessibility

Citizens in parishes far from a TAJ branch face expensive travel costs and full days out of work just to access a basic government service — creating an unequal system.

📍 Disproportionately affects rural residents
Problem 03
🕵️

Fraud Through Manual Checks

Manual document comparison by officers is inconsistent and cannot reliably detect sophisticated forgeries. AI verification removes this vulnerability entirely.

📍 A systemic security gap in the process
Why Use DLRSJAM?

Built around your life,
not around a branch schedule.

Whether you are a busy professional, a rural resident, or just someone who values their time — DLRSJAM was designed with you in mind.

🏠

Renew from Anywhere

No travel, no time off work. Apply from your phone, tablet, or computer — at home, at the office, or anywhere with an internet connection and a camera.

⚡

Minutes, Not Hours

AI verification runs in seconds. Submit your application and wait for officer approval — there is no queue to stand in, and no day wasted at a branch.

🛡️

More Secure Than Manual

Face matching, liveness detection, and OCR cross-referencing work together to verify your identity with a level of accuracy that visual inspection simply cannot match.

🪪

Your Licence, Instantly

Once a TAJ officer approves your application, your digital driver's licence is ready to download immediately — with a QR code for on-the-spot verification anywhere.
