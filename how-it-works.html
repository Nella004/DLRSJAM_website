System Overview

One platform,
three user roles.

DLRSJAM is a role-based Progressive Web Application that brings together three distinct user types — each with their own dashboard, permissions, and workflow — all connected through a single secure platform.

"The AI handles the heavy lifting. The TAJ officer makes the final call. The citizen gets their licence — from home, in minutes."

No app store downloads. No dedicated hardware. Any modern smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera and internet connection is all a citizen needs to complete the full renewal process.

Step Three

AI Identity
Verification

This is the core of DLRSJAM. Three separate AI modules run automatically the moment a citizen submits their application — Tesseract.js reads the document text, face-api.js compares the ID photo against a live selfie, and a TensorFlow.js model confirms the selfie is from a real, live person.

🔍
OCR Text Extraction — Tesseract.js reads every field on the uploaded licence and cross-checks details against the application form at 98.2% accuracy.
👤
Facial Recognition — face-api.js generates a 128-point face descriptor and computes a similarity score between the ID photo and the live selfie.
🎥
Liveness Detection — a TensorFlow.js model checks for blink patterns and micro-movements to confirm the selfie is not a photo or screen replay.

Step Four

TAJ Officer
Review & Approval

Every application — regardless of its AI confidence score — passes through a TAJ officer before any licence is issued. The officer dashboard presents the AI results in a clear, structured format. Officers can approve, reject, or flag an application for further review, and can override AI results when professional judgment requires it.

📊
AI-assisted review — officers see confidence scores, OCR output, and face match results side by side, saving significant review time.
🔁
Human override — the AI assists but never replaces. Officers retain full authority to approve or reject regardless of AI results.
📝
Audit trail — every decision is logged with a timestamp, officer ID, and written notes for full accountability.

Under the Hood

The AI verification engine,
explained.

Three independent AI modules work in parallel the moment an application is submitted, each verifying a different dimension of the applicant's identity.

🔍
OCR Document Reading
Tesseract.js
Tesseract.js extracts all text content from the uploaded licence image using optical character recognition. Each field — name, licence number, expiry date, address — is parsed and automatically cross-referenced against the data the citizen entered in their application form.
Extraction Accuracy

Facial Recognition
face-api.js
face-api.js generates a 128-dimensional face descriptor for both the photo on the uploaded ID document and the live selfie captured during the application. The cosine similarity between the two descriptors produces a percentage match score, flagging any result below the configured threshold for officer review.
Face Match Score
94.7% avg.

128 landmarks
Method

Liveness Detection
TensorFlow.js
A TensorFlow.js model analyses the selfie video stream to confirm the person is physically present in front of the camera — not a printed photo or a screen recording. The model checks for natural eye blinks, micro-expressions, and depth of field cues to confirm liveness before any facial matching can proceed.

Security Design

Designed with security
at every layer.

DLRSJAM was not built with security as an afterthought — it was baked into the architecture from the first line of code. From the way passwords are stored to the way documents are transmitted, every element of the platform follows established security best practices.

Crucially, human oversight is itself a security feature. The AI never makes the final call — a real TAJ officer reviews every application before a licence is issued. This hybrid model combines the speed and consistency of AI with the judgment and accountability of a human institution.

🔐
Encrypted Passwords
bcrypt hashing ensures stored passwords are never recoverable, even in a breach.
🎫
JWT Sessions
JSON Web Tokens enforce role-based access on every protected API endpoint.
🔒
HTTPS Everywhere
All data in transit is encrypted — documents, selfies, and form data never travel in plain text.
📋
Full Audit Trail
Every officer decision and system action is logged with timestamps for full accountability.
