What Is DLRSJAM?

A digital renewal system built for real Jamaicans.

DLRSJAM, the Digital Licence Renewal System Jamaica, is a working prototype developed by five University of the West Indies students as their COMP3901 Capstone Project.

The system was designed to answer a genuine gap in Jamaica's digital government services: there is currently no official way to renew a driver's licence without physically visiting a TAJ branch.

"Jamaica has been successful in creating a digital government framework via the Government of Jamaica's Digital Transformation Strategy; however, driver's licence renewal remains unaddressed in the digital space."

DLRSJAM demonstrates what a modern, secure, and accessible licence renewal system could look like using AI-powered identity verification, a structured officer approval workflow, and a digital licence delivered straight to your device.

💡

The Problem Identified

Manual TAJ visits, long queues, rural inaccessibility, and fraud risk create daily hardship for Jamaican drivers needing to renew their licences.

🌍

Global Inspiration

Countries like Estonia and the UK already use OCR scanning, face matching, and liveness detection for digital ID renewals. Jamaica has the infrastructure to do the same.

🏗️

The System Built

A Progressive Web App (React + Flask) with three AI modules: OCR, face matching, and liveness detection integrated with a structured TAJ officer approval workflow.

🪪

The Result

A fully functional prototype that takes a driver from application to downloadable digital licence without a single visit to a branch.

The Problem

Three failures.
One broken process.

The current TAJ driver's licence renewal system was built for a pre-digital era. These are the three core failures it creates for Jamaican citizens today.

Problem 01
⏳

Hours Lost in Queues

Every renewal demands a physical visit to a TAJ branch. Long wait times are the norm; citizens spend hours in lines just to hand over documents that could be submitted digitally in seconds.

⏱ Directly affects every single Jamaican driver
Problem 02
🗺️

Rural Inaccessibility

Citizens in parishes without a nearby TAJ branch face expensive, time-consuming travel to access a basic government service creating an unequal and unfair system for rural Jamaicans.

📍 Disproportionately impacts rural communities
Problem 03
🕵️

Document Fraud Risk

Manual comparison of documents leaves the process vulnerable to forgeries. Visual inspection is inconsistent and cannot reliably detect sophisticated tampered submissions.

🔍 A systemic security gap with no current solution

Our Solution

So we built the solution ourselves.

DLRSJAM is a Progressive Web Application meaning it works on any smartphone, tablet, or computer, with no app download required. It combines proven AI libraries with a structured human approval process to create a system that is both powerful and trustworthy.

The AI handles the heavy lifting reading documents, matching faces, and checking liveness — while a real TAJ officer retains final approval authority on every application.

🔐

AI Identity Verification

Three AI modules verify who you are automatically OCR, face matching, and liveness detection working in parallel

🏛️

Human Officer Oversight

Every application is reviewed and approved by a TAJ officer, the AI assists, it never replaces human judgment

🪪

Instant Digital Licence

Approved applications generate a downloadable PDF licence with a QR code for verification available immediately

📱

Works on Any Device

No app download needed, the PWA runs in any modern browser on phones, tablets, and computers
